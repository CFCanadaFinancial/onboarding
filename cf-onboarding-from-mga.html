Plain-language glossary

Broker of record
The MGA/agency recognized by a carrier as holding your contract. The transfer changes this designation to CF.
AGA transfer
Associate General Agent transfer — the carrier paperwork moving your contracting between agencies.
In-force block
Your existing policies and the renewal/trailer compensation attached to them.
Charge-back
Commission clawed back when a policy lapses early — relevant when advances exist at your current MGA.

A note on accuracy

Transfer requirements, release policies, and compensation treatment vary by carrier and contract, and they change. Every item on this page is confirmed by CF staff for your specific file before you act on it.
